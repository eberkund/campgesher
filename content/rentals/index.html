Camp Gesher has a lot to offer for rental groups. Our delicious food, great land and water facilities, variety of cabins, vast number of indoor and outdoor meeting locations, our convenient location, and the overwhelming beauty of our grounds make Camp Gesher a great choice for any retreat. On top of all our facilities we recognize that not everyone has the same needs, which is why we are open to any form of customization.

We offer a lot of land facilities for your enjoyment. For the sporting types we provide a basketball court, soccer field, and baseball diamond. We also have a new jungle gym, several swing sets, and a big sand pit.

We have approximately 30 cabins to fit a wide range of needs. At Camp Gesher we pride ourselves on constantly upgrading our cabins while still preserving the rustic homey vibe of wooden cabins.

We are fortunate to not only be situated on pristine Lake Pringle, but also have many great toys to make the most of it. We have a water trampoline and water slide. We also offer canoeing, kayaking, and paddle boating.

All our cabins have brand new wooden porches, some extending the entire length of the cabin. Our most recent endeavour was a brand new modern cabin built with huge windows and high ceilings. We also have several larger cabins that have bathrooms.

Camp Gesher is conveniently located three hours from Toronto and Montreal, and two hours from Ottawa. We’re also just ten minutes away from Bon Echo Provincial Park.

Our kitchen can provide Kosher catering services. Our experienced kitchen team can accommodate almost every dietary need too. Whether your group is big or small, our team is available to service your needs.

Availability

Rental availability times range from the middle of May to the end of June, and from the middle August to September. To inquire further, or get a quote please email info@campergesher.com.
